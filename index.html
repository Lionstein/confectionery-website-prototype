Founded in 1812, Beau's Bonbons is Canada's oldest French candy chain. Starting out in a maple shack in Northern Quebec, Beau's Bonbons delicious maple candy recipes soon made it a staple in any Canadian home. By the 1900s, Beau's Bonbons had grown to include popular candies from across Canada, and by the 1950s, we had candy from all over the world!
